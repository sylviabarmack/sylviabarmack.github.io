Childhood

DJ Khaled was born on November 26, 1975 in New Orleans to Palestinian parents. His parents were immigrants and exposed him to music at a young age, playing a lot of Arabic music. However Khaled from an early age became interested in rap and soul music. He had one brother Alla Khaled.

Signature Sayings

DJ Khaled is known for yelling "DJ Khaled" in the back of his songs. He is also known for a few other sayings. Some of these include:"We the best!", "Major key", "They don't want me to succeed", "Anotha one", and "Bless up". He can be spotted saying catch phrases and generally flexing on his notorious Snapchat stories.

Rise to fame

How did DJ Khaled get famous?

In the early '90s DJ Khaled aquainted Lil Wayne and other rappers. These friendships proved to be a "major key" in his success. He met them at Odyssey, an New Orleans record store where he worked. After leaving Odyssey he began DJing and experimenting with a style of music that seemlessly combined hip-hop and dance music. He began to aspire to be involved in radio DJing. His first radio gig was on a pirate radio station. In 1998, He moved to Miami where he co-hosted the radio show "99 Jamz". In 2003 he got his own weeknight radio show on "99 Jamz".
