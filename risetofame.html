Rise to fame

How did DJ Khaled get famous?

In the early '90s DJ Khaled aquainted Lil Wayne and other rappers. These friendships proved to be a "major key" in his success. He met them at Odyssey, an New Orleans record store where he worked. After leaving Odyssey he began DJing and experimenting with a style of music that seemlessly combined hip-hop and dance music. He began to aspire to be involved in radio DJing. His first radio gig was on a pirate radio station. In 1998, He moved to Miami where he co-hosted the radio show "99 Jamz". In 2003 he got his own weeknight radio show on "99 Jamz".
